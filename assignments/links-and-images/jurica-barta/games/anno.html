Anno

"Anno" is a series of real-time strategy games developed by Ubisoft Blue Byte and released since 1998.
The games are set in different historical eras, and players take on the role of a ruler tasked with building and managing a prosperous city.
The gameplay involves resource management, city planning, and diplomacy with other factions.

Each "Anno" game features detailed graphics and a unique setting, such as the industrial revolution or the future.
The games also have a strong emphasis on exploration, with players discovering new islands and trading with other cities to expand their empire.
The series has evolved over the years with new gameplay mechanics, updated graphics, and improved AI.

"Anno" is known for its deep and challenging gameplay, allowing players to create their own unique cities and societies.
The game requires careful planning and strategy, as well as effective communication with other factions, to succeed.
The series has a dedicated fan base and is considered one of the best real-time strategies of all time.
